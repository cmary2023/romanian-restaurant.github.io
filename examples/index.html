Welcome to Restaurant Moldova

We all miss our country. We miss the air we breathed there, we miss the smell of Romania, our parents, brothers, sons and daughters.
We know how hard it is to leave behind the house, the city, friends, family and go to a totally unknown place.
Precisely because we know all these things, we try to make up for the lack by offering you another thing that you will surely miss, namely ROMANIAN FOOD.
The quality and taste of our dishes will make you feel at home, at least for the moment.
We invite you to enjoy the ROMANTIC taste with us, big or small.
